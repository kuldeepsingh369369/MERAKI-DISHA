Express your interest

Tell us a bit about yourself and how you would like to contribute. We will get back to you within a few days to find the best way to involve you.

Do I need to be based in Uttarakhand?

No. Many roles including design, communications, fundraising, and digital mentoring can be done from anywhere in India or abroad.

How much time do I need to commit?

There is no fixed requirement. Even a few hours a month can make a meaningful difference. We will work around your availability.

Can students or freshers volunteer?

Absolutely. We welcome volunteers of all experience levels. If you are passionate about education and children, there is a role for you.

Will I receive any documentation?

Yes. Regular volunteers can receive a certificate of contribution from Mirai Society upon request.
