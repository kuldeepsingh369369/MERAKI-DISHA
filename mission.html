Flagship initiative

Mirai Learning Center

Many children across India remain enrolled in school but cannot attend regularly — due to distance, family responsibilities, economic pressures, or other barriers. Mirai Learning Center gives these children a place to learn every day, while keeping them connected to their school for exams and official progress.

Why it matters

What this model changes

For children, families, and schools — the Mirai Learning Center model creates outcomes that no single approach can achieve alone.

Children stay officially enrolled

Students keep their school enrollment intact, sit for exams, and earn qualifications — without losing their right to formal education.

Learning happens every day

Instead of falling behind, children continue their education at the center with structured, teacher-led daily sessions.

Schools reach more students

Partner schools can support children they would otherwise lose to dropout, strengthening community-wide educational outcomes.

Partner with us

Register your school's interest

If your school has students who are enrolled but cannot attend regularly, we would like to work with you. Register your interest and our team will be in touch within a few days.

Which students can be registered?

Any student who is officially enrolled in your school but faces barriers to regular attendance — due to distance, family circumstances, economic constraints, or other reasons.

How do exams work?

Students remain enrolled at your school and appear for all exams there as normal. Mirai Learning Center handles their daily education in between.

Is there a cost for schools?

We are committed to keeping this accessible. Please reach out and we will discuss arrangements based on your school's situation.

Where is Mirai Learning Center located?

Currently operating in Uttarakhand. We are expanding across India — register your interest even if you are outside our current area and we will get back to you as we grow.
