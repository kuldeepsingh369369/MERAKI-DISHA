About Meraki-Disha

"Meraki" (Greek) means: To do something with soul, creativity, or love. "Disha" (Sanskrit) means: Direction or Path. Together, it stands for guiding with passion and purpose.

Meraki-Disha is an NGO committed to nurturing the potential of underprivileged students. We believe that many students from marginalized backgrounds possess incredible talent; what they often lack are resources, mentorship, and opportunity.

Our core aim is to become a bridge between their immense potential and a brighter future, fostering growth not just in knowledge, but in soul.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To ignite a generation of learners who grow not just in knowledge, but in soul—discovering purpose, expressing creativity, and walking their own path with courage and compassion.

Our Mission

  • To create soulful learning spaces where knowledge meets imagination, and children feel safe to dream boldly.
  • To empower youth as mentors and changemakers in their communities.
  • To blend science, stories, and service into every child’s journey of self-discovery.
  • To replace fear with freedom, pressure with passion, and isolation with belonging.

What are Meraki Corners?

Meraki Corners are innovative, interactive science and creativity zones established right inside schools. These dedicated spaces are designed to move beyond traditional textbook learning.

In Meraki Corners, students actively engage in practical learning through hands-on models, exciting experiments, and collaborative team projects. This approach fosters a deeper understanding and a love for discovery.

Our primary aim with Meraki Corners is to foster scientific temper, encourage innovation, and ignite a boundless sense of curiosity in every child.
