Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Mirai Society may receive, how it may be used, and how you can contact us with any privacy-related questions.

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Information we may receive

If you contact Mirai Society by email, phone, WhatsApp, or social media, we may receive the information you choose to share with us, such as your name, phone number, email address, social media profile details, and the content of your message.

Purpose of use

We may use this information to respond to your questions, coordinate support, communicate about volunteering or partnerships, and stay in touch regarding the work of Mirai Society.

Consent and communication

Where consent is relevant, we aim to use personal information only for the purpose for which it was shared with us. If you no longer wish to hear from us, you may ask us to stop using your contact details for future communication.

Sharing of information

Mirai Society does not sell personal information. Information shared with us is used only for communication, coordination, and support related to our work, unless sharing is required by law or necessary for a trusted service used by us.

Children’s information

Mirai Society works in support of children. If we ever collect personal information relating to a child directly through forms, registrations, or future digital services, we will take additional care and, where appropriate, work through a parent, guardian, school, or responsible adult.

Retention and deletion

We try not to keep personal information for longer than necessary for the purpose for which it was shared. If you want us to review, correct, or delete your personal information from our communication records, you may contact us directly.

Third-party services

This website may link to social platforms, YouTube, WhatsApp, or future donation providers. Those services have their own privacy policies, and their handling of data is outside the direct control of Mirai Society.

Rights and grievance contact

If you want to ask what information has been shared with us, request correction or deletion, withdraw consent for future communication, or raise a privacy-related concern, please contact us at miraisocietyuttarakhand@gmail.com.

Updates to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time as the website grows, donation methods are added, or communication processes change.
